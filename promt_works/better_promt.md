Here are the official channels for contacting the Prime Minister's Office (PMO) of India:

## Online

- **Official Website**: [pmo.gov.in](https://pmo.gov.in) — has a "Connect with PM" or "Write to PM" section
- **CPGRAMS Portal**: [pgms.gov.in](https://pgms.gov.in) — the Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System for submitting grievances online

## By Post

**Office of the Prime Minister**
South Block, Raisina Hill
New Delhi - 110011
India

## By Phone

- **PMO Helpline**: 011-23012312
- **Fax**: 011-23016857

## Social Media

- **Twitter/X**: @PMOIndia
- **Facebook**: PMO India (official page)

## Tips

- For formal correspondence, a written letter sent by post is still a respected channel.
- CPGRAMS is the most efficient route if you have a specific grievance — it's tracked and assigned a registration number.
- Responses can take time depending on the volume and nature of your query.
